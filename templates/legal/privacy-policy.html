Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Simply Law ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Simply Law website and consultation platform. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and otherwise handle your information when you use our services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information you provide directly, including:

  • Name, email address, and phone number
  • Company name and business information
  • Legal issue descriptions
  • Documents you upload for consultation
  • Payment information (processed through Paystack)

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and improve our consultation services
  • Process payments and send confirmations
  • Communicate with you about your consultations
  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Prevent fraud and provide security

4. Information Protection

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

5. Client Confidentiality

Communications with our lawyer are protected by professional attorney-client privilege. We do not disclose confidential client information except as required by law.

6. Third-Party Services

We use Paystack for payment processing. Paystack's privacy practices are governed by their privacy policy. We do not share your information with other third parties without your consent.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. Contact us at info@simplylaw.com to exercise these rights.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the last updated date.
